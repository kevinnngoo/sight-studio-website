Meet Dr. Anna Ngo

Meet Dr. Anna Ngo, the practice owner of Sight Studio! Dr. Ngo began her educational journey at Western New England University, where she earned a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration. Upon graduation, Dr. Ngo felt a deep calling to healthcare and pursued a premedical post-baccalaureate program at Mount Holyoke College. This path ultimately led to the completion of her Doctor of Optometry degree from the Chicago College of Optometry.

Throughout her career, Dr. Ngo has gained valuable experience working alongside both optometrists and ophthalmologists in Connecticut, Massachusetts, Illinois, and Florida treating ocular disease and providing specialized care for a wide range of patients. Driven by a passion for improving vision and eye health, Dr. Ngo is committed to providing personalized care and innovative solutions for patients.
