Committed to delivering personalised eye care that makes a difference.

Meet Dr. Anna Ngo

Dr. Anna Ngo began her academic journey at Western New England University, earning a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration. Driven by a passion for healthcare, she transitioned to a premedical post‑baccalaureate program at Mount Holyoke College before completing her Doctor of Optometry at the Chicago College of Optometry.

Over the years, Dr. Ngo has worked alongside optometrists and ophthalmologists across Connecticut, Massachusetts, Illinois and Florida, gaining broad experience in treating ocular disease and caring for diverse patient needs. She believes that clear vision begins with attentive listening and patient education.

Today, as the owner of Sight Studio, Dr. Ngo is dedicated to building lasting relationships with her patients and ensuring everyone receives the highest standard of care.
