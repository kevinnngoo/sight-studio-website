Services

Welcome to Sight Studio!

At Sight Studio, we're dedicated to providing personalized, high-quality eye care for the whole family. Whether you're visiting us for a routine exam or specialized services, our experienced team is here to help you see clearly and maintain lifelong eye health. Explore the services we offer below:

Comprehensive Eye Exam

Our comprehensive eye exam evaluates your vision and overall eye health. We assess your prescription needs and screen for common eye conditions like glaucoma, cataracts, and macular degeneration—helping you maintain clear, healthy vision at every stage of life.

Eye Exam

Contact Lens Fitting

Our contact lens fitting ensures you find the perfect lenses for comfort, clarity, and eye health. We offer a full range of options—including spherical, astigmatic (toric), and multifocal lenses—customized to your vision needs and lifestyle. Whether you're new to contacts or need an updated fit, we've got you covered.

Contact Lenses

Diabetic Eye Exam

Our diabetic eye exam focuses on detecting early signs of diabetes-related eye conditions, such as diabetic retinopathy. Using advanced imaging and a thorough retinal evaluation, we help protect your vision and support your overall health with timely, expert care.
